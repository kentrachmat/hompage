Music

When we talk about music, you're talking about my life.

I have studied music since I was 4 years old. At first, I learned classical piano however everything changed when I finished my sophomore year, I met Jazz music.

Since that, learned lots of things such as improvising, play as a group, etc. I'm pretty proud of what i'm capable of, yeah I know I'm not a child prodigy but I did several events, for instance : I played in some music festival like Tanjung Perak Jazz Festival and Jazz Traffic Festival, got into music magazine, obtained the highest score in Indonesia in (jazz examination), gig at the hotel to play jazz lounge music, and many other things.
