Hello! I'm Kent.

a jazz pianist with a profound love for music that began at the tender age of five. My musical journey has been enriched by my experiences with my school band and the Elmynt Trio, where we performed together in various gigs.

My formal music education includes attending music schools like Rapsody and Melodia. However, a significant turn in my playing style was shaped at Jazz Centrum in Surabaya under the mentorship of Yason Gunawan, and in Bali with the legendary Indonesian jazz musician Indra Lesmana. Further honing my skills, I studied at the Conservatoire de Lille, where I was mentored by Cyrille Wambergue.

Highlights

  • Rhapsody Spring Music Festival 2007: 2nd Place
  • Inter-School Religious Singing Competition 2014: 3rd Place
  • Featured in Staccato Magazine as a Talented Young Pianist 2014
  • ABRSM Jazz Performance 2016: Highest Score
  • Performance with Indra Lesmana at Jazz Traffic Festival 2018
  • Played with a group on a Provincial Television Show 2018
  • Featured Artist at Tanjung Perak Jazz Festival 2018
  • Performances at Shangri-la Hotel Jazz Duet 2018-2019
